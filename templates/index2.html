Trends Observed in Benefits Offered From 2011-2014 in the San Francisco Job Market

Benefits offered in the job market in San Francisco in 2011 appear to be virtually none. However, as the years progress, we see an increase of benefits offered with jobs. This progression shows with each year. From this, we can infer that the job market in San Francisco is improving with time, in offering benefits.

Upon viewing total pay over the years, some outliers close to an annual income of $550,000 in 2011 have been observed. In 2014, outliers at $400,000 and $500,000 were observed. Not counting the 2 outliers in 2011, a consistent trend showing increase in annual income can be shown over the years. From here, we can infer a trend in better job prospects over the years.
